For Maths Teachers

Easy instructions how to ogranize Maths Battles and Maths Circle in your school

Books

If you read it most probably you passion about making maths education better. How to motivate the students to love maths? How to teach them not just formulas but mathematical way of thinking? How to create a sistem that will day after day help them to advance their maths skills?

We would like to share our experience of organizing Maths Battles and Maths Circles. Maths Battles is a competition that is unlike other teach students mathematical way of thinking. They shouod not only solve the problems but also explain their solutions and find inaccuracy in other solutions. Plus it is a team competition and a lot of fun. Competitive and social aspect of maths battles motivates students. We recommend organizing them every 2-3 months. Moths circle is a weekly activity where the students learn advanced maths.

On this page you will find information that will help you to organize Maths Balltes and Maths Circle in your school.
